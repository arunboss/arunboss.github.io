About DeepView

FotoBot came into being as a small project for a friend. It slowly became a suite of products spawning into what is today DeepView Analytics. Our vision is help businesses run a state-of-the-art analytics stack with minimum oversight. What you see is what you get, period!

It has been a habit of old to rely solely on community driven, open source packages under pypi, MIT licenses, BSD licenses etc. Once the core idea was implemented successfully, we sensed an opportunity to fill up a vacant space in the market currently run by ad-hoc solutions, since Big Tech are out of reach for common folks! Key among these, not exhaustive:

  • pypi/face_recognition
  • pypi/pillow
  • scikit
  • Viper
  • J Walter Weatherman
  • Cast
