Use Case Story

There are a group of use cases that blend nicely with the subject of computer vision. We will stay at 10,000 feet with respect to outlining these for interested users. More details are given in connected sections. This is based on where we see most traction in terms of market needs. Future releases will be listed in subsequent sections.

Broadly, all major industries & SME segments will have an interest in this subject. We have listed out the below industry segment for the purpose of our reading:

Retail

  • SKU management, customer management, billing, fraud

Automotive

  • Driverless car, autonomous drive

Security

  • Attendance, tracking, surveillance, secure area

Healthcare

  • Diagnosis, record, analyse, routine inspection for advance diagnostic

Event Management

  • Brand outreach & engagement, customisation

Banking

  • KYC, Digital, Fraud

FinTech

  • KYC, Loans, Fraud

Industrial

  • Remote, Inspection, Diagnose health
